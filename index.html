ARRMapper Field Tool

A mobile-ready web application for field data collection on Afforestation, Reforestation & Revegetation (ARR) carbon projects. Map plantation saplings, assess soil types, capture GPS boundaries, and sync field data directly to project Google Drive — all from a phone.

🌱 Plantation MapperSpecies-based sapling placement on a grid with shapefile & map export
🧪 Soil MapperHand-drawn soil type polygons with area calculation and classified export
📐 Vector ToolGPS & manual POI, path, and polygon capture with KML export
☁️ Drive SyncAutomatic upload to project Google Drive on every export
